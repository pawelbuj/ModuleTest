Digital Workers

Not a chatbot. A worker inside your process.

A Digital Worker knows the case state, the rules, the roles, and what it’s allowed to do. It prepares, suggests, and executes — with full audit trail.

U

Understand

Reads emails, forms and documents. Extracts fields and intent. Turns noise into data.

A

Act

Creates cases, assigns owners, triggers steps, and prepares responses and documents.

E

Escalate

Asks humans when context matters. Uses approval gates and logs every action.

How it works

Start small. Scale fast.

You don’t need a big transformation. Pick one process. Put a Digital Worker on the first shift. Expand based on measurable outcomes.

1
Pick one painful flow

Support intake, approvals, HR requests, invoices — a real bottleneck with real volume.

2
Teach your Digital Worker

Provide examples, rules and approval gates. The agent learns your language and boundaries.

3
Measure, then expand

Track time-to-decision and automation rate. Move more work to agents — safely.

Control

Automation with accountability.

Digital Workers operate inside roles and rules. Humans keep control with approvals. Everything is logged — so you can audit and improve.

R

Role-based access

Same rules for people and agents. Clear permissions, predictable behavior.

G

Approval gates

Let agents prepare and suggest. Require humans to approve where it matters.

L

Full audit trail

Every action is recorded: who/what did it, when, and why.
