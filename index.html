Why it matters

AI without process becomes a liability.

Chat can help, but it doesn’t own the work. There’s no queue, no accountability, no history, no gates. You need a process layer that can prove what happened.

What breaks today
!
Work gets lost between tools

Email, Teams, spreadsheets, tickets, calls… nobody has the whole picture.

!
Manual handoffs kill speed

Copy/paste, forwarding, “can you check this?” — delays and mistakes multiply.

!
No governance for AI actions

If an agent makes a wrong move: who approved it, who is responsible, what changed?

What 2to2 changes

Every request becomes a tracked case — with a next step.

2to2 turns scattered inputs into a single flow. Agents take the first shift: read, extract, route, prepare actions — humans approve where it matters.

Orchestration

People + agents. One workflow. Clear control.

Agents are not “free to do anything”. They act inside states, permissions and approval gates. Everything is logged. That’s the whole point.

R

Role-based permissions

Same rules for people and agents. No surprises. No “agent did something random”.

G

Approval gates

Let agents prepare and suggest. Require humans to approve high-risk actions.

L

Full audit trail

Who/what did it, when, and why — tied to the case state and data changes.
